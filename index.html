About This Project

This website was created as part of a Doctor of Audiology capstone project. Its purpose is to support clinicians in providing culturally competent care to Spanish-speaking patients.

Communication barriers can affect patient understanding, counseling, treatment adherence, and overall hearing healthcare outcomes. This resource provides practical tools and guidance to help audiologists navigate language differences and deliver equitable care.
