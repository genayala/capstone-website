About this project

Why this exists and how to use it
Purpose

Support culturally competent audiology care for Spanish-speaking patients by reducing communication barriers, improving counseling clarity, and offering topic-based materials designed for real clinic flow.

Who it is for

English-speaking audiologists and hearing healthcare providers who regularly serve Spanish-speaking patients and families (with or without interpreters).

How to use

Go to Resources, filter by topic (HA, CI, Protection), and open a resource card. When your PDFs are ready, link them in one place and the site becomes fully downloadable.

FAQ

Quick answers (edit these anytime)
Do I need to upload my flyers right now?

No. The site is designed to look complete with “Coming soon” placeholders. Add PDFs when ready.

How do I add a PDF later?

Upload it to assets/pdfs/ and update the “Download PDF” link inside that resource card.

Can this be shared publicly?

Yes. GitHub Pages is a public link. Avoid uploading anything with patient identifiers or clinic-proprietary content.
