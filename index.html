About

Purpose, scope, and how to use this site

This capstone website supports audiologists and hearing healthcare providers in delivering culturally competent care to Spanish-speaking patients and families. The goal is to reduce communication barriers, improve counseling clarity, and provide practical tools that fit real appointments.

Equitable access
Culturally responsive counseling
Language support
Downloadable patient materials

What this includes

Topic-based handouts and educational flyers (English + Spanish, as needed), plus tools for interpreter-supported visits.

Who this is for

Clinicians who primarily use English but need reliable resources for Spanish-speaking patients and caregivers.

How to use it

Go to Resources, pick a topic (hearing aids, CI, hearing protection), download materials, and adapt to your clinic.
